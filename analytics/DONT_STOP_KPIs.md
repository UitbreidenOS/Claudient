# DONT STOP KPIs

## Overview
Success metrics for DONT STOP framework tracking goal achievement, process efficiency, financial discipline, and user satisfaction.

---

## Primary Success Metrics

### 1. Goal Success Rate
**Target:** 80% by Month 1

**Definition:** Percentage of enrolled goals that achieve their stated objective within the planned timeframe.

**Measurement:**
- Track goal completion status (achieved/missed)
- Calculate: (Goals Achieved / Total Goals Enrolled) × 100
- Exclude goals marked as abandoned or deprioritized by user consent

**Threshold:** ≥80% indicates framework is effective at driving goal closure

---

### 2. Approval Time Violations
**Target:** <10% of submissions

**Definition:** Percentage of goal approvals that exceed the committed SLA.

**Measurement:**
- Log submission timestamp and approval completion timestamp
- Calculate: (Approvals Exceeding SLA / Total Approvals) × 100
- SLA baseline: 24 hours for standard goals, 48 hours for complex goals

**Threshold:** <10% keeps process efficient and maintains user momentum

---

### 3. Budget Overrun Rate
**Target:** <5% of goals

**Definition:** Percentage of goals that exceed their allocated resource budget.

**Measurement:**
- Track budgeted resources (time/money/tokens) vs. actual consumption
- Calculate: (Goals Over Budget / Total Goals) × 100
- Include: time allocation, compute costs, API costs, team capacity

**Threshold:** <5% ensures financial discipline and predictability

---

### 4. Net Promoter Score (NPS)
**Target:** >50

**Definition:** Measure of user satisfaction and likelihood to recommend DONT STOP.

**Measurement:**
- Survey users monthly: "How likely are you to recommend DONT STOP? (0-10 scale)"
- Calculate: % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
- Segment by: goal type, experience level, framework adoption stage

**Threshold:** >50 indicates strong product-market fit and user satisfaction

---

## Secondary Metrics

### Goal Velocity
- **Definition:** Average time to reach goal completion from enrollment
- **Target:** Trending downward month-over-month
- **Action:** Identify blockers causing delays

### Approval Quality
- **Definition:** % of approved goals that require revision within 7 days
- **Target:** <15%
- **Action:** Improve submission template if exceeding threshold

### User Adoption Rate
- **Definition:** % of active users completing onboarding checklist
- **Target:** ≥75% by Month 1
- **Measurement:** Track onboarding step completion

### Goal Type Distribution
- **Definition:** Breakdown of goal categories enrolled
- **Target:** Balanced across domains (no single category >40%)
- **Action:** Identify underserved domains, adjust positioning

### Feature Usage Frequency
- **Definition:** % of users engaging with each DONT STOP module
- **Target:** ≥60% usage per core feature
- **Measurement:** Weekly active user counts per feature

## Measurement Schedule

| Metric | Frequency | Owner | Report |
|--------|-----------|-------|--------|
| Goal Success Rate | Weekly | Analytics | Dashboard + Monthly Summary |
| Approval Time Violations | Daily | Ops | Weekly Alert if >10% |
| Budget Overrun Rate | Weekly | Finance | Dashboard + Escalation if >5% |
| NPS | Monthly | Product | End-of-month Report |
| Secondary Metrics | Weekly | Analytics | Internal Dashboard |

---

## Alert Thresholds

**Red Alert (Immediate Action):**
- Goal Success Rate drops below 70%
- Approval Time Violations exceed 15%
- Budget Overrun Rate exceeds 8%
- NPS drops below 40

**Yellow Alert (Review Required):**
- Goal Success Rate between 70-80%
- Approval Time Violations between 10-15%
- Budget Overrun Rate between 5-8%
- NPS between 40-50

---

## Review & Iteration

- **Monthly:** Review all KPIs, adjust targets if needed
- **Quarterly:** Deep-dive analysis, identify systemic issues
- **Annually:** Reset targets based on maturity and market conditions

## Success Definition
Framework achieves **success at Month 1** when:
- ✅ Goal Success Rate ≥80%
- ✅ Approval Time Violations <10%
- ✅ Budget Overrun Rate <5%
- ✅ NPS >50
